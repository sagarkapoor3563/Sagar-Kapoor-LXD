I am an Instructional Designer dedicated to crafting engaging, learner-centric experiences. Leveraging frameworks like ADDIE and SAM, I transform raw instructional content into interactive digital courses that drive performance and behavioral change.

My approach combines cognitive psychology, user experience design (UX), and interactive multimedia to create accessible learning paths for diverse audiences.
